Want a second set of eyes on it?

Sanity-checking wedding timelines is what I do every weekend. Send me your date and your draft run sheet, and I will tell you honestly where it is tight, where it is generous, and where the light will be doing you favours. No obligation, just a photographer who has seen a few hundred of these play out.

Sunset times are calculated for the centre of your chosen region and shown in your device's local time, so they are accurate to within a few minutes. Travel estimates are yours, so be honest with them, and remember a wedding day version of any drive takes longer than the Tuesday version. Durations are based on how real days tend to run, not best-case scenarios.
